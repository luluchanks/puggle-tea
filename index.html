ABOUT US: PUGS, PURITY, & PASSION


A portion of our monthly profits go towards the Pug Lives Matter Foundation for the rescue efforts of pugs across America. Each month we tell the story of a pug you're helping just by enjoying our products!

We value purity in all of our ingredients and products. Everything used to make our teas are organic, high-quality, and homemade. Our products are made to not only taste good, but also make you feel good.

We are passionate about our products, our business, our customers, and our pugs. We want everyone to have the best experience possible starting from when you first enter our doors to when you finish your tea!
